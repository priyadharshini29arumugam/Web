With the recent Hydraulics sale to Danfoss,we are forming the New Danfoss Power Solutions to
become an even stronger global leader in mobile and industrial hydraulics,serving our customers and

We are Stronger Together.
We are the NEW Danfoss Power Solutions.

This is a new, exciting chapter for both our businesses - the bridging of two very strong
hydraulics manufacturers.

Together we are creating a global leader in mobile and industrial hydraulics.

    We both have common roots – a long, proud history in hydraulics. Our product and market coverage are complementary, with the most comprehensive product

    engineers to partner with and a localized sales force to serve our customer even better than
    before.
